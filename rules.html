Server Protocol

Everything you need to know to stay out of the gulag.

🚨 Critical Security

1. Spying & Unauthorized Access TERMINATION
Any attempt to spy on staff communications, social engineer access, or intercept private server data is strictly prohibited. Consequences:
  • Permanent IP & UUID Ban.
  • Immediate revocation of Panel/Console access.
  • Blacklist from all associated staff platforms and projects.
2. Credential Sharing Bannable
Sharing staff account details or panel logins with 3rd parties will result in immediate termination of access and a network-wide ban.

🎮 Gameplay

1. Griefing & Theft Bannable
Don't touch what isn't yours. If you didn't build it or grow it, leave it alone. We track block history—don't bother lying about it.
2. Client Modifications Bannable
Standard utility mods (JEI, Optifine, Sodium) are fine. X-ray, Fly, and KillAura will get you nuked by the anti-cheat.
3. Lag Inducement Warning
Optimize your redstone. Zero-tick farms or massive entity cramming setups that tank the TPS will be deleted without a refund.

💬 Communication

1. Toxicity & Harassment Bannable
Banter is fine, but hate speech and targeted harassment are instant exits. We aren't here to babysit drama.
2. Advertising Mute/Ban
Don't post links to other SMPs or Discord servers. We aren't your free billboard.
